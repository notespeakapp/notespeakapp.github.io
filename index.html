About the Founder

How this app was created and developed

My name is Yingzhi Ma and I come from Beijing, China. I have been living in America for two years. One of the problems I came across as a student was that I couldn't understand my teachers in class since English is not my first language. I noticed many of my classmates and friends had a similar problem too. That's when it clicked, I could create a note-taking application! Since then, I have worked hard to develop and deliver this creation to you. I expect to continuously work on and improve Notespeak, and that it may help you in class, work or any other situation you may need it for.

Hands-Free Utility

Nowadays many school teachings are very long, and keeping track of all information is difficult. Sometimes teaching goes way too fast for the students to take down all notes. This app will help to solve this problem. Next time your instructor starts their lesson, you can start your app and have it register everything they say word by word, and after taking notes, it will show the user some explanation with pictures that can help the user understand more.
